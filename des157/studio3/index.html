Welcome to the Game of Pig!

  1. Roll the dice when it's your turn. The number you roll gets added to your score.
  2. If you roll a one, the turn passes to the next player.
  3. You can also choose to pass your turn to the next player.
  4. Be careful! If you roll two ones, or 'Snake Eyes', your score gets set back to zero!
  5. The first player to reach a score of 30 wins!
